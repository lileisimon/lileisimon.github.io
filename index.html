About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Logistics and Maritime Studies at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University since August 2023. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Purdue University in 2023, advised by Prof. Qi Annabelle Feng and Prof. J. George Shanthikumar. I obtained the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Management from Zhejiang University, in 2015 and 2017, respectively, avdivsed by Prof. Qingwei Jin.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students with strong interests in supply chain and operations management. Students with a bachelor/master degree in operatoins management, operations research, industrial engineering, (applied) mathematics, probability, statistics, physics, computer science, or other related fields are welcomed to email me with your CV and transcript. Competitive financial support will be provided (see https://www.polyu.edu.hk/gs/prospective-students/fellowship-scholarship-schemes/).
